# Before / After

## Before

```text
用户：帮我做一个会员支付页面
AI：好的，我开始写代码...
```

Common problems:

- Requirements are not clarified
- MVP scope is unclear
- Edge cases are missed
- No acceptance criteria
- No handoff or test steps

## After

```text
用户：Use $pm-dev-workflow to build a membership payment page.
AI：
1. Clarify user, payment methods, membership benefits, and success signal
2. Point out login, payment failure, order state, refund, and analytics risks
3. Define MVP scope and out-of-scope items
4. Plan files, implementation order, and test steps
5. Build, self-test, and hand off with clear verification instructions
```

## Result

The AI is no longer just coding. It is helping move a vague request into a shippable product task.
